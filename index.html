Everything about flags!

Here, you have a collection of flag-related information, resources and games. If you want to learn more about flags or get your friends interested in the topic, this is a great starting place.

The History of Flags

Flags have been used for centuries to communicate messages, represent nations, and symbolize movements. From the banners of ancient armies to the flags of modern countries, each flag tells a story. Discover how flags have evolved and learn about the meanings behind their designs.

Flag Design: Art Meets Identity

What makes a flag memorable? Is it the bold colors, the meaningful symbols, or the simplicity of its design? Learn about the principles of flag design and how flags blend creativity with cultural significance. Who knows? You might be inspired to create your own!

What Can You Find Here?

  • Discover Flags: Learn about the flags of the world and their unique stories.
  • Make your own: Visit our external partner site where you can craft your very own flag with easy-to-use tools.
  • Take the Quiz: Test your flag knowledge with an interactive quiz. Can you recognize flags from around the globe?
